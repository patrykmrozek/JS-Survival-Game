ENDLESS MODE

Welcome to "Landalorian's Haunted Escape: Ghostlight Conquest," where you'll step into the boots of a lone Landalorian warrior trapped within the eerie confines of a haunted house. Equipped with a powerful flashlight mounted atop your helmet, you have the advantage of controlling its direction without needing to physically turn your head, thanks to its automated swivel mechanism. As you navigate through the spooky confines of the house, waves of vengeful ghosts will test your mettle. Your survival depends on your wits, reflexes, and strategic use of your flashlight to ward off the encroaching darkness and spectral threats. Are you ready to face the unknown and conquer your fears in this thrilling adventure?


[CONTROLS & TIPS]

W A S D - to move

LEFT MOUSE BUTTON - to shine flashlight

KILL GHOSTS - to recharge flashlight

PICKUP HEALTHPACKS (yellow squares) dropped from ghosts - to regain health

PICKUP POWERUPS (rainbow squares) dropped from ghosts - to gain 1 of 3 random powerups

LONG FLASH: doubles the distance covered by the flashlight for a short while

EXTRA DAMAGE: doubles the damage done by the player for a short while

WAVE WIPE: creates a shockwave that kills all enemies in the current wave
